AI Art Charm Jigsaw Privacy Policy
Last Updated: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to AI Art Charm Jigsaw. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play our mobile application and ensures transparency regarding our data practices.

2. Information Collection and Use
To provide game features, manage your progress, and support in-app purchases, we collect the following information:

User Identifiers: We collect unique identifiers (such as Firebase UID) to manage your account, save your gold coin balance, and track game progress.

Financial Information: We track your purchase history via Google Play services to ensure your in-app purchases and virtual currency (Coins) are correctly saved and applied.

Device Information: We collect non-identifiable information such as device model and OS version to optimize performance and fix bugs.

Advertising ID: We use the Google Advertising ID to display advertisements and perform analytics via AdMob.

3. AI-Generated Content (Grok AI)

Image and Video Content: All portrait images and videos in this app are generated by artificial intelligence (Grok AI).

Nature of Content: These images are purely fictional representations and do not depict real individuals.

Compliance: We ensure that all AI-generated content adheres to Google Play's policies regarding safe and appropriate content.

4. Data Deletion and Security

Data Deletion: Users have the right to request the deletion of their account and all associated data. You can request deletion by contacting us at hoaihubdev@gmail.com or by following the instructions in our Account Deletion Page.

Security: Data is transferred over a secure connection and encrypted during transit to ensure your information remains protected.

5. Third-Party Services
We utilize the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own policies:

Google Play Services: For achievements and in-app purchase management.

Firebase (Google): For real-time database synchronization and analytics.

AdMob (Google): For delivering personalized or non-personalized advertisements.
